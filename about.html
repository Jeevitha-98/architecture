Who We Are

From a small workshop to full-scale architectural stewardship.

Founded in 2012 by a passionate team of architects and designers, STACKLY Studio began as a small boutique workshop focused on bespoke residential projects. Over the past decade, we have evolved into a leading architectural firm known for our innovative approach to design, commitment to sustainability, and dedication to client satisfaction. Our journey from humble beginnings to industry leaders is a testament to our unwavering passion for creating spaces that inspire and endure.
